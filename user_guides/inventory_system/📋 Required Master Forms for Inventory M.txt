## ⚠️ Global Validations (All Master Forms)

- **Unique Codes:** All codes are unique per company (or globally where specified)
- **Inactive Block:** Inactive records cannot be selected in new transactions
- **Delete Protection:** Records referenced in any transaction cannot be deleted — only deactivated
- **Audit Trail:** All creates, edits, status changes logged with user + timestamp
- **Approval on Sensitive Changes:** Bank details (vendor), credit limits, tax rates require dual approval
- **Naming Convention:** All codes: UPPERCASE, no special characters except hyphen and underscore
